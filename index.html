National Student Research Institute (NSRI)

The National Student Research Institute is a student-driven initiative focused on research, innovation, and collaboration among young researchers. The institute supports student projects in technology, science, and education while encouraging independent thinking and practical experimentation. NSRI aims to build a community where students can develop skills, create projects, and share knowledge.
