About NSRI

NSRI is a global research pipeline that connects high school students, undergraduates, and PhD-level mentors from prestigious institutions like MIT, Emory University, and Oxford into one structured system for producing real research. We're breaking down barriers to research access and helping students transform their curiosity into published work.

"Most students don't lack ability—they lack access, structure, and mentorship. NSRI was created to solve that problem."

What You'll Get

Join NSRI and unlock world-class research opportunities

Expert Mentorship

Step-by-step guidance from experienced researchers including MIT's Dr. Leo Celi and 10+ accomplished professors until your paper is published.

Research Training

Comprehensive training from idea formation to final submission, including methodology, data analysis, and scientific writing.

Free Conferences

Access to online research conferences year-round with talks from ISEF winners and top student researchers worldwide.

Publication Pathways

Direct pathways to major journals (JEI, Lumiere, Curieux) and competitions (ISEF, Conrad Challenge, Regeneron STS).

Flexible Timeline

Work at your own pace with structured milestones and support. Perfect for balancing with school commitments.

Quality Assurance

PhD-level mentors ensure research quality and guide you through peer review and publication standards.
