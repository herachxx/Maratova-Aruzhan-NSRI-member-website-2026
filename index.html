About Aruzhan

Aruzhan Maratova is a Grade 10 student at Aqbobek International School (AIS) in Aktobe, Kazakhstan, and currently serves as the NSRI Program Director for the AIS chapter — one of the fastest progressions in the program's history. With a deep focus on cybersecurity, embedded systems, and competitive programming, Aruzhan is systematically building the expertise and portfolio needed for her goal: studying cybersecurity at MIT. Global Research Pipeline for Students

NSRI connects high school students, undergraduates, and PhD-level mentors into one structured system for producing real, publishable research. Founded by a student affiliated with MIT Critical Data — because most students don’t lack ability, they lack access. NSRI exists to close that gap, one chapter at a time.
